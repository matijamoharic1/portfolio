EcoTrip — Designing a more sustainable travel experience

EcoTrip is a mobile travel app concept that helps users discover and book lower-impact trips with less friction, clearer guidance, and more relevant recommendations.

Challenge

Travelers often face too many generic options, making it difficult to compare choices and confidently select eco-conscious alternatives.

Approach

  • Reduced cognitive load with a simpler onboarding flow
  • Highlighted sustainable options earlier in the journey
  • Designed a fast, mobile-first browsing experience

Solution

Personalized destination recommendations, stronger sustainability cues, and a cleaner structure that helps users scan, compare, and act faster.

Reflection

The project strengthened the balance between clarity and inspiration, showing how thoughtful structure can improve usability without losing visual warmth.

35% Faster booking flow
50% Stronger visibility of eco options
25% Higher onboarding completion

Tiskly — A clearer 3D packaging design experience

A web platform concept for uploading artwork to 3D box models, helping users preview packaging with more confidence before production.

Bridging the gap between 2D design files and 3D product previews

Tiskly simplifies the design-to-print process by giving users a more direct and understandable way to upload, position, and preview packaging artwork on 3D models.

The goal was to reduce friction, lower the chance of print errors, and create a more intuitive workflow for users who need visual feedback before placing an order.

Problem

  • Switching between multiple tools slowed the process
  • It was difficult to imagine how flat artwork would translate into 3D
  • Limited visual feedback increased the risk of mistakes

Solution

  • Real-time 3D preview of uploaded designs
  • Simple artwork upload and placement flow
  • Clear controls for adjusting layout and positioning

Design approach

A calm, practical interface focused on clarity first: fewer distractions, immediate feedback, and a layout aligned with modern e-commerce expectations.

Outcome

A more confident validation process, reduced uncertainty before production, and a smoother bridge between concept, preview, and final order.
