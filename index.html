EcoTrip — Designing a Clearer Travel Experience

A mobile travel concept focused on simplifying decision-making through clearer structure, better prioritization, and more accessible eco-friendly options.

Problem

  • Travel platforms often present too many choices at once
  • Sustainable options are difficult to identify and compare
  • The booking experience can feel fragmented and slow

Approach

  • Simplified hierarchy to reduce visual noise
  • Focused user flows that support faster decisions
  • Stronger emphasis on key actions and useful information

Solution

  • Home screen for quick entry and personalization
  • Main browsing screen for structured discovery
  • Detail page for confident comparison and booking

Outcome

A more focused travel flow with clearer navigation, improved usability, and a smoother path from discovery to decision.

Design Focus

Simplicity, clarity, and a visual structure that helps users move through the experience with less effort.

Key Features

Personalized entry points, structured listings, and an intuitive screen-to-screen journey built for mobile use.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly choices are surfaced more clearly, making responsible decisions easier to notice and understand.

Result

A cleaner concept with stronger hierarchy and three polished screens that communicate the product direction more effectively.

Tiskly — 3D packaging design experience

Tiskly is a web platform concept that allows users to upload and preview their designs directly on 3D packaging models before production.

The project focused on making the design-to-print process feel more visual, more intuitive, and less error-prone for users who need confidence before placing an order.

Problem

  • Designing packaging required switching between different tools
  • It was difficult to imagine how flat design would look in 3D
  • Limited preview increased the risk of mistakes

Solution

  • Real-time 3D preview of applied artwork
  • Simple upload and placement workflow
  • Clear positioning controls and product details

Design Approach

A practical, clarity-first interface with minimal distractions, immediate visual feedback, and a layout that feels familiar and modern.

Outcome

A smoother validation process, fewer surprises before production, and a stronger connection between concept, preview, and final product order.
