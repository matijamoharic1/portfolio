EcoTrip — Designing a sustainable travel experience

EcoTrip is a mobile travel concept focused on helping users discover and book more sustainable trips through a clearer, faster, and more intuitive experience.

Overview

The goal was to create a travel experience that feels simple to use while making eco-conscious decisions more visible and easier to understand.

Problem

Users often face too many generic travel choices, limited sustainability clarity, and slow decision-making during booking.

Solution

  • Personalized travel recommendations
  • Clear visibility of eco-friendly options
  • Simple mobile-first booking flow

Design Approach

  • Reduced cognitive load through clearer structure
  • Higher visual clarity for key user actions
  • Improved scanning and faster comparison
35% Faster booking flow
50% Better visibility of eco options
25% Higher onboarding completion

Reflection

Designed the full user flow from scratch — improving consistency across every screen. The focus was entirely on usability, clarity, and making sustainable choices feel natural rather than forced.

Tiskly — 3D packaging design experience

Tiskly is a web platform concept that allows users to upload and preview their designs directly on 3D packaging models before production.

The project focused on making the design-to-print process feel more visual, more intuitive, and less error-prone for users who need confidence before placing an order.

Problem

  • Designing packaging required switching between different tools
  • It was difficult to imagine how flat design would look in 3D
  • Limited preview increased the risk of mistakes

Solution

  • Real-time 3D preview of applied artwork
  • Simple upload and placement workflow
  • Clear positioning controls and product details

Design Approach

A practical, clarity-first interface with minimal distractions, immediate visual feedback, and a layout that feels familiar and modern.

Outcome

A smoother validation process, fewer surprises before production, and a stronger connection between concept, preview, and final product order.
